NEURO PATHWAYS

Enhance Neurofeedback in Brisbane provides a unique service where we assess your individual needs and integrate neurofeedback with other methods such as counselling,  coaching and group training to assist you with creating new pathways. Research has shown children and adults of all ages see dramatic improvement with Learning Difficulties, ADHD, Depression, Anxiety, Brain Injury, Migraines, Seizures, and Chronic Pain. Neurofeedback is an exciting therapy and peak performance tool. The technology to influence brainwaves means that we can improve our functioning and capacity in many areas in a lasting way.
